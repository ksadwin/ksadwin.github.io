Linguistic Self-Expression on Dating Websites

An in-progress, undergraduate research project with Beth Dellea that seeks to identify idiosyncratic features of language on the dating website OkCupid. Over 1500 user profiles have been scraped and tagged with gender, sexual orientation, location, and age as reported by the user on the website. The full text of profiles and their tags were stored in a SQLite database and analyzed for linguistic features using Natural Language Processing (NLP). The most concise and engaging results appear when the profiles are visualized using word clouds grouped by the tags.

Findings were presented at the New York Celebration of Women in Computing (NYCWiC) and the James J. Whalen Academic Symposium in Spring 2016, where Dellea and Sadwin were finalists for awards.

Read the results here!

  • Date: Fall 2015-Spring 2016
  • Platform: Python, SQLite

Sadwin's Very Complicated Price Guidelines for Knitting

  1. Horizontal stripes are free. Any other colorwork (vertical stripes, monogram, fair isle patterns) will cost an additional 25-50% of the base price of the item, pending on complexity.
  2. Stitches other than stockinette or garter run 25-75% pending on complexity. For instance, it might be 25% for a simple rib, 50% for cabling, and 75% for lace patterns.
  3. If a custom pattern is needed, this may have an additional charge related to size/complexity of the project.
  4. You may be eligible for a discount if you choose a yarn color already in Sadwin's inventory! Narrow it down to a few shades before inquiring about available yarns because otherwise we'll both be here all night.
  5. Yarn weight (thickness) will also affect pricing. Projects using bulky yarn will be reduced in price by 25%; projects requiring finer yarns may be increased in price by up to 50%.
  6. Listen this is sounding like hand-wavy nonsense so if you're curious about getting something done just email me and we'll work something out together.
